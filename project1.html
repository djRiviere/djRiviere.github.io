PROJECT 2: Battleship

Concept

Battleship

This is the standard game of battleship. Placing ships on a field and in this case playing against a bot that tries to aim at your ships and shoot them down. The field contains one 5 block ship one 4 two 3’s and one 2. the whole purpose is that you find and shoot down the enemy ships before the bot shoots all of yours.

How i did it

This project was something I had to do in quite a relatively short time and I underestimated how hard it would be. I had to make an AI that would shoot down and track ships and how good I wanted the AI to be all came down to how much time I had. I also had to make an interface. Be able to place ships over that interface and make sure ships couldn’t be placed over each other or out of the interface. I made this project mainly by using my previous knowledge of lists and decided to use two dimensional lists to make the field and place the ships. I doubled down on my weakness of not knowing functions in C# and decided to focus on that. To limit the amount of code I needed to type. That said it still wasn’t all automated with it being such a big task. But the functions I made were essential to the game. It made me learn how functions and functions within functions can pretty much automate anything.

What i learned

In this project i learned allot about not only the syntax of C# but also how to use what i know to eliminate some of my weaknesses on a code which was basically forreign to me. It also helps because C# was the language we would use most in the future. and the faster i would get into it the more i would be able to keep up. there was allot i still didnt know. but after getting into it. learning more about C# became allot easier because i got a general idea of the language and how it works.
