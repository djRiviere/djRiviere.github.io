Dion Riviere

Software developer

I'm 20 years old. And right now I'm studying to become a back-end software developer. I started doing software development after I decided to pick it up because my father did the same thing. And found that it was fun to do even in my free time. There are rarely things that get my attention like that so I decided to start studying at SintLucas. I'm very ambitious and have big goals in life. I want to start my own business at a certain point and am trying to learn more about investing to increase my income. When it comes to software development I'm most interested in complex problem solving and working with algorithms. Hence why I'm looking of becoming a back-end software developer.

Personal Goals:

why, how and experience or what i learned

INVESTING

I made battleship because it was always a game i played on the nintendo. and the thing i like most about software development are algorithms. they are by far what interrests me most. solving complex problems and trying to make something with it. What really drove me to make this project was also making a working bot. and figuring out how i would manually make everything. i always love deconstructing things and ideas so i can figure out how to make something myself. altough i dont have much programming or technical projects to show for it. i always deconstructed things regarding my hobbies. like how does my muscle memory is sports work? and how can i improve my learning regarding that? Thats why i made this project.

MODELLING

I had already made sketches of how to make the project earlier. and took my experience from the previous project to work out those ideas further. I decided to take everything i learned how to do. and use it to its full extent and with it improved on the feedback i got from last time and also went deeper into that too. the only issue with that is that my mind is only focussed on what i can use to do what i want instead of looking for a completely new better alternative to sloving the problem. Even so. i majorily used arrays which i knew quite well. and expanded on my knowledge

BUSINESS

What i learned is to first expand on my knowledge on things i dont know. rather than creatively finding ways to use what i already know. its easy for me to do creative things with what i know. but learning a new method for code, is just like jumping out into the unknown for me. i have to make a really big project and having to learn something new first feels overwhelming and takes time. but in the broader view of things. if i first looked at what i didnt know when starting this project instead of instantly starting with the creation of it. i would probably have increadibly solid code whitout many flaws and no bugs. ofcourse it wouldnt be perfect. but it wouldve been so much easier in the longrun. so next time. i have to learn how to judge what the best course of action is even before i start
